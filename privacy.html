Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Overview

TriDo ("the App") is a productivity application developed by Humans AI. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

The short version: We collect nothing. Everything stays on your device.

Data Collection

TriDo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers are used
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No account creation is required

Local Data Storage

All data you create in TriDo (tasks, settings, streak history) is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Notifications

TriDo may send local notifications (morning reminders) if you enable them. These notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

TriDo does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

TriDo does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all TriDo data by uninstalling the app. No additional action is needed as no data exists on any server.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at krishna@humansai.io.
