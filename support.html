Frequently Asked Questions

Why can I only add 3 tasks?

That's the point! Research shows your brain holds 3-4 things in working memory. By limiting to 3, you focus on what truly matters and actually finish your tasks instead of endlessly growing a list.

Where is my data stored?

Everything is stored locally on your device. No cloud, no accounts, no servers. Your tasks never leave your phone.

How do streaks work?

Complete all 3 tasks in a day to maintain your streak. The day resets at your configured time (default 4:00 AM). If you miss a day, the streak resets to zero.

Can I change the daily reset time?

Yes! Go to Settings and tap "Reset time" to choose when your day rolls over. Night owls often set it to 4 AM or 6 AM.

How do I export my tasks?

Go to Settings and tap "Export tasks." This creates a plain text list of all your completed and incomplete tasks with dates.

Will there be a Pro version?

We're considering adding premium features like custom themes, widgets, and streak recovery in a future update. The core 3-task experience will always be free.
